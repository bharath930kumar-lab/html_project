NETWORKS

Different types of (private) networks are distinguished based on their size (in terms of the number of machines),
their data transfer speed, and their reach. Private networks are networks that belong to a single organization.
There are usually said to be three categories of networks:

  • LAN (local area network)
  • MAN (metropolitan area network)
  • WAN (wide area network)
